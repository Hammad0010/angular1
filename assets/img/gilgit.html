Gilgit-The Beauty of the North

Pakistan is blessed with breathtaking locations in Northern areas of the country which will definitely make you fall in love with nature. Here we go with our list of highly recommended destinations that wanderlust must visit at least once in our lives.

Deosai Plains

Deosai is located on the boundary of the Karakoram and the Western Himalayas and it is one of the highest plateaus in the world standing at 4100meters and is spread over 3000sq.km.

Rama Meadow

Rama Meadow is ahead of Rama Village and is almost 11 KM away from Astore district; which is one of the largest districts of Gilgit-Baltistan.

Shangrila resort, Skardu

In the north of Pakistan, Skardu the significant valley of GB is a perfect amalgam of serenity, beauty, and wilderness.

Gojal Valley

Gojal Valley borders with China at Khunjerab and is at 15,397 feet above sea level.

Attabad Lake

Attabad Lake is a beautiful lake in the ‘Gojal Valley,’ Hunza of northern Pakistan that was created in January 2010 by a landslide dam.

Nature & Parks in Gilgit-Baltistan

Gilgit striking sceneries, fecund gardens and plentiful parks, ensure its dwellers and visitors are never far from the nature. Hiking, Biking, Boat rides, Bird Watching, Hill climbing, morning/evening walks are some of the options to discover Gilgit nature

Deosai National Park

If you are planning to go via Astore , first visit Minimerg are after crossing Burzail pass and then come back to Chelum chowki and go till Sheosar Lake and if have some time go till Bara pani. It is a full day exhausting adventure .

Nanga Parbat

If you are a trekker, an adventure lover or interested in seeing the landscapes, trek to Nangaparbat is a complete package for you. Your trip starts with a thrilling jeep ride from Karakorum Highway and reach Tattu village.

K2 Mountain

Pakistan has some of the best trekking in the world and several of the world's 2th highest mountains, including k2.Many people visit the country just to trek and the tourism industry is well prepared to facilitate.

Gilgit Shopping Malls

If you are a shopaholic and you have never had the chance to wander through the busy marketplaces of Gilgit then trust us when we say that you have been missing out on everything. So, here is a quick peek at some of the best shopping markets in Gilgit to help you get out and about.

The Forum Mall

It is Gilgit’s largest and most innovatively planned shopping complex with a majestic structure overall. This exemplary retail hub is located in Johar Town is like a city within a city as it is home to 200+ brands along with a special food court of most sought after restaurants.

Mall of Gilgit

Mall of Gilgit is a high street shopping destination which provides a touch of serenity to the shoppers. Located at the prime location of Gilgit Cantonment on Mall Road, it promises a great shopping experience with an array of high-end fashion apparel for people.

Fortress Square

Fortress Square is a 3-storey shopping arcade featuring top designer brands. It is located at the Main Boulevard Gulberg – the mid of Gilgit. It’s a single destination with the best of the best designer apparel for people. Along with top most footwear brands.
