Lahore would not be finished without a night at the Wagah fringe. This place denotes the fringe amongst Pakistan and its neighbor, India.

Lahore Museum is outstanding amongst other spots to find out about the intriguing history of Pakistan.

Lahore Parks

Lahore striking sceneries, fecund gardens and plentiful parks, ensure its dwellers and visitors are never far from the nature. Hiking, Biking, Boat rides, Bird Watching, Hill climbing, morning/evening walks are some of the options to discover Lahore nature

Zoo Safari Park

safari park is a recreational wildlife park spread 242 acres. It was a government-funded public facility until it was handed over to the Punjab Wildlife and Parks Department (PWPD). The zoo has a diverse variety of species.

Jallo Park

Jallo is another wildlife covering an area of over 450 acres and having a possession of some of the most common mammals, birds and reptiles. Their additional attractions include boating, water slides and fishing.

X Park

X Park offers exclusive services like Go-kart rides, wall climbing, jumping, free running, ATVs, mini motos, etc. The sporting facilities are available for people of all group ages including children, teenagers, middle-aged.

It is Lahore’s largest and most innovatively planned shopping complex with a majestic structure overall. This exemplary retail hub is located in Johar Town is like a city within a city as it is home to 200+ brands along with a special food court of most sought after restaurants.

Mall of Lahore is a high street shopping destination which provides a touch of serenity to the shoppers. Located at the prime location of Lahore Cantonment on Mall Road, it promises a great shopping experience with an array of high-end fashion apparel for people.

Gulberg Galleria

Gulberg Galleria is a 3-storey shopping arcade featuring top designer brands. It is located at the Main Boulevard Gulberg – the mid of Lahore. It’s a single destination with the best of the best designer apparel for people. Along with top most footwear brands.
